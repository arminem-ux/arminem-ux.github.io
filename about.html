Get To Know Me

Hey,
I am Armine. User experience designer based in Yerevan, Armenia.
For the past 11 years I’ve been working in the field of design, striating off as a print graphic designer and moving forward to web design till finding my true passion in UX.

What makes UX so fascinating for me?
It’s the challenge, it’s the problem solving, it’s like one of those quest like puzzle games where you have to gather queues and find the right keys. There is no one solution that fits all. For each product, each use case, each micro interaction you need to find that perfect solution.
The part I like the most is the moment you find that perfect tailored interaction, and when you do you instantly know it. It’s the one, the key you were looking for, the missing puzzle. It’s like a revelation.

Now A little about my interests…
I’m very passionate about art. I do some occasional painting once I have a little spare time. In past few years I rediscovered the power and pleasure of reading.I’m having a hard time categorizing my reading lists so it’s basically a mixture of UX, Psychology, Science, and Art. I believe understanding users cognitive patterns plays a big role in the design process so lately I went ahead and got my CBT ( Cognitive behavioral Therapy ) Coach certificate and am planning on gaining a more in-depth knowledge in the field. So that’s one of my goals for 2020.

What I do to relax and have fun?
I love jogging in the morning, I enjoy hiking and long walks, I love exploring new unique places and delicious meals (I just love food :) and finally I just love to cuddle and spend some quality time with my two kids and my two not so well behaved doggies.
